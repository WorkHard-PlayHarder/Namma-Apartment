You don't have to be superhuman, you just have to believe in yourself to be a real hero.

What does raise the film a few notches and make it probably one of the better films directed by Ratner is its revisionist take on the tale of Hercules. Loosely adapted from Steve Moore's comic book series, the film has Dwayne 'The Rock' Johnson as the mythical son of Zeus. However, instead of the superhero we expect, he is a mercenary who uses the tales of his divine parentage and superhuman labours to woo new clients and scare prospective enemies.
